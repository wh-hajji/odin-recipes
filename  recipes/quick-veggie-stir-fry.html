Quick Veggie Stir-Fry

Description

Here is a quick and delicious vegetable stir-fry recipe that's perfect for a busy weeknight. Loaded with colorful vegetables and flavorful sauce, this dish is a healthy and satisfying option for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce and sesame oil. Pour over the vegetables and toss to combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and serve immediately. Enjoy your quick veggie stir-fry!
