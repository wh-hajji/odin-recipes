One-Pot Pasta

Description

Experience the simplicity of a delicious one-pot pasta dish. With minimal effort and easy cleanup, this recipe combines your favorite pasta with a flavorful sauce, creating a perfect harmony of taste in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups penne pasta
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot, saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add penne pasta and cook according to package instructions, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once pasta is cooked, stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and combined.
  6. Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your easy and delicious one-pot pasta!
